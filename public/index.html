Horse Combat For Horsemanity

Start How To Play Credits

A battle of worlds has commensed. The horse captains of the Light and Shadow worlds are in combat to decide who shall rule the horse planet. It is up to you to defeat your opponent!

Two players use the same computer to play. Choose to be the Light Horse and the Shadow Horse amongst yourselves. You will see a letter float above your character. As you press the letter, a new letter will appear. Keep pressing letters until you have gained total control. A winner emerges when one side has complete control, as indicated in the power bars on the top of the screen.
